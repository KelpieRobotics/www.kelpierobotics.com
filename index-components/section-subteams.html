Sub-Teams

As engineering students, we recognize that collaboration and teamwork is critical to overall success.
We have four subteams:

Business

The business subteam deals with sponshorships, funding, and general logistics of operating the team.

Electrical

The electrical subteam deals with the electrical aspects of the ROV from motors, to controllers, to power distribution.

Mechanical

The mechanical subteam deals with the design and manufacturing of the ROV enclusre, as well as interior mounts. They also ensure that the ROV is able to withstand the necessary forces, and that it remains water tight.

Software

The software subteam deals with programming the various microcontrollers, as well as setting up the software suites on the topside, and ROV computers.
