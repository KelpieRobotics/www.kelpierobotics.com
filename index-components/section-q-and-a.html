Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is an ROV?

    An ROV is a Remotely Operated Vehicle which is normally sent into hostile environments for humans. This could be anywhere from the darkest depths of the ocean to the surface of Mars. It consists of a robotic vehicle remotely controlled by a human, and is typically equipped with video cameras, and mechanical attachments to accomplish the designated task.

  • What competition does Kelpie Compete in?

    Kelpie Robotics competes in the MATE ROV competition. Competition organizers specify various tasks, such as manipulating underwater objects and using visual measurement tools, and teams design their ROVs to best accomplish these tasks.

  • What is the purpose of the ROV?

    The purpose of the ROV is to allow for exploration of difficult environments without compromising human operator safety and at a lower cost. Submersible ROVs, for example, can replace divers in certain scenarios.
