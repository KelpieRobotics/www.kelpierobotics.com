Welcome to Kelpie Robotics Web Page

Here at Kelpie Robotics, we pride ourselves in giving ROV building our best shot. Our team consists of students from various degress, and with our combined skills, form a well rounded team.

  • We have interests in underwater robotics
  • We have interests in developing the necessary equipment for underwater deployment
  • And we have interests in the logistics of running a team that deals with underwater robotics

The whole team started off with a small group of students who decided to get together and pitch the idea of an underwater robotics team to the university. This involved rapidly developing and testing a prototype unit which took about a week, and then successfully pool testing it. Following this and a few logistics issues, the University allowed the team to be officially formed leading us to today.
